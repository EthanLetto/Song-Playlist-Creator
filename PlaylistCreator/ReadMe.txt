Install instructions: Install npm 

Launch instructions: Go to the location of server.js in the terminal and enter “node server.js” to execute the program

Testing Instruction: While running “node server.js”, visit “http://localhost:3000/mytunes.html", "http://localhost:3000/mytunes", "http://localhost:3000/index.html", "http://localhost:3000/" or "http://localhost:3000", and enter a song in the search area (example: Body and soul). Then add the song to your playlist by pressing the "+" button. Move your songs around in your playlist by pressing the arrow buttons and remove songs by pressing the "-" button.
